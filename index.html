About this site

This site contains pictures and some relevant materials of model locomotives which have been constructed over thirty years. Since yours sincerely ia only interested in either steam or electric locomotives, no other type of locomotives were made. Currently, thirty one modells are opend here. ( Adding more is not very likely,... )

Most of all the locomotives are scaled by 1/45 which makes 1435mm to 32mm, so called O gauge.

The reasons that the O gauge is selected are, not many people make by this scale, simpler to construct by full scratch, looks impressive even if construction is not in super detailed and so on. All of them are motor driven, digitally controlled with no comaptibility with somebody else's system whatsoever.

Detailed design plans are rarely available and dimensions of many of the modells are obtained by observing many pictures, thus some of them are not quite in scale. Elevation drawings are found in PDFs.
